R.K. NARAYAN

The Guide by R.K. Narayan is a story of Raju, a tour guide who evolves from a cunning businessman to a revered spiritual leader. The novel explores love, ambition, deception, and redemption, set in the fictional town of Malgudi. It highlights the contrast between illusion and reality, making it a profound study of human nature and transformation.

The Guide by R.K. Narayan is relevant for competitive exams, especially in literature, UPSC, and NET exams, due to its themes, narrative style, and cultural significance.
