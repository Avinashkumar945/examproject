Jawaharlal Nehru

*The Discovery of India* by Jawaharlal Nehru is a historical and philosophical exploration of India's rich cultural heritage, history, and identity. Written during Nehru’s imprisonment (1942–1946), the book delves into India's past, the impact of colonial rule, and the vision for an independent nation.

*The Discovery of India* is relevant for competitive exams, especially for UPSC and other civil services exams, as it provides deep insights into Indian history, culture, and the freedom struggle.
